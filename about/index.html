about

This is intended to be a website to host The Dorsal, a student run newspaper at Pittsburgh Science and Technology Academy. The primary intentions of this "newspaper" will be to:

  1. provide a space for students to publish their ideas and opinions about the school
  2. allow for *any student* to practice the skills of leadership, journalism, data analysis, writing, and/or photography
  3. be financially self-sustaining (in terms of server, email, and domain costs)
